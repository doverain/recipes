Almond cookies
1lb almond flour  (or make your own - ljp)
4 eggs whites
2 1/4 powder sugar, (I use less)
1 tsp. baking powder

Heat the oven to 350 - 375F.  Beat egg whites with powder sugar until foam is created.
Add baking powder, and almond meal 
Mix it.

Cook for 10-12 min.
